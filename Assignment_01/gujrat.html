KHAKRA
Khakhra is a thin cracker common in the Gujarati cuisine of western India, It is made from mat bean, wheat flour and oil. It is served usually during breakfast.

PATRA NI MACHHI
Patra Ni Machi is a very popular Parsi dish in which fish smeared with generous amount of green chutney and then wrapped in banana leaf and steam cooked. Serve with steamed rice and Vegetable Dhansak.
