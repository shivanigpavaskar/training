SHUKTO
Shukto is a popular vegetable dish in Bengali cuisine usually served with rice in the West Bengal state of India and in the neighbouring country Bangladesh.

DOI LLISH
Doi Ilish is a signature Bengali Ilish Mach preparation that is widely enjoyed in the region. It is prepared with fresh Hilsa fish chunks that are cooked in a yogurt-based sauce.
