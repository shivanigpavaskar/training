AVIAL
Avial is an Indian dish with origins in the Kerala state of India. Avial is considered an essential part of the main meal in Kerala (Oonu in Malayalam) and is also served as a delicacy in South India.

AADU ATTIPATHAL
Aadu Attipathal is among the most loved mutton dishes in all of Kerala cuisine, with its hot flavour and juicy texture making the most of the base protein.
